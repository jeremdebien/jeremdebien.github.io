Kare - Kare

Kare-kare is a popular Filipino dish made of a thick savory stewed peanut sauce. It is generally made from a base of stewed oxtail, beef tripe, pork hocks, calves feet, pig's feet or trotters, various cuts of pork, beef steak and occasionally offal.

Sisig

Sisig is a Filipino dish that is usually made of meat and various ingredients in a single plate. Calamansi, onions and chili peppers are normally used to season it. Sisig is regarded as one of the most popular Kapampangan dishes which makes it widespread across the whole country.
